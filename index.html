Welcome to the Sudoku Solver, you can let the solver try to solve it for you, if it fails to do so please click the I'll do the rest button.

Or you have the chance to the just enter the given inputs and start solving it by yourself, with the help of the solver warning you a given integer won't go in the place, if you were to make a false entry.

In order the assign a new integer to a already filled square, you must empty it first.

You can click show possibilities button to see what is happening in the background.
